SREE GURUDEVA CENTRAL SCHOOL 

Sree Gurudeva central school was established in the year 2002 with the objective affordable and quality education to the 
children of rural areas.The school was affiliated to the central board of secondary education,New Delhi in 2006.In 2008,
the school was upgraded to senior secondary level.The school provides quality education for more than 1500 students with the 
help of more than 100 qualified, efficient and well trained teaching and non teaching staff.
